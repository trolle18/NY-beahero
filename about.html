ABOUT

Be a Hero is a small and personal business with a lot of experience, owned by Benthe Elisabeth Jørgensen. Be a Hero offers coaching of people of all ages and walks of life and helps them achieve a work/life balance.

Do you need help transitioning from your current position to a more demanding one, then Be a Hero is the right place to help. Does your staff need help creating a better work/life balance, so they can work more efficiently, then we are the place to go.

Benthe has over 20 years of experience with positions such as international leadership in commercial, coaching, training, learning and development in pharmaceuticals, medtech, recruiting and staffing.
