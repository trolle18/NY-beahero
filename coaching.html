Coaching

Coaching is a journey of self-discovery and development.
As a certified professional ACC coach I will help you develop your strengths and identify your values. This will lead to a more balanced and fulfilling life with increased energy and happiness.

I offer:

  • Coaching within areas of development, talent, performance, career, stress or burnout


  • Facilitation of workshops


  • Learning and Development sessions to maximize talent and performance


  • Coaching Skills for Leaders


  • In person and/or online sessions




What is the difference between Values and Strengths?
Values is something we have. Our core Values works as our inner compass.

Strengths are something we are. Strengths are our core Values in action. The more we use our strengths, the happier we become. The science about Strengths derives from Positive Psychology.

Positive psychology is the scientific study of what makes life most worth living - Peterson, 2008.

Using our top Strengths on a daily basis makes us feel stronger connected to our purpose, and consequently we do more of the things which gives us energy.



This is a positive spiral helping us in many situations.

We become stronger, more impactful and increasingly smarter in our interactions with others, when using our Strengths deliberately. It's effortless to use our Strengths in terms of energy, and the extraordinary thing is, we are energized when using our Strengths.

When identifying our most important Values and Strengths, we are uncovering our ethics and natural behavior. It becomes easier to navigate in our lives and relations, when being aware of our Values and Strengths.

Our core Values is our inner compass helping and guiding us to the right decisions and actions.
